Welcome to the University of Future.

=== GENERAL INFORMATION ===
Office timings are Monday to Friday 9 AM to 5 PM.
The campus is closed on weekends and public holidays.
The main campus location: 123 Future Ave, Tech City, Mars Colony 4.
Contact number: +1-555-0199.
Email: info@future.edu.

=== ADMISSIONS ===
The registration fee is $50 USD, non-refundable.
To apply, visit our website at www.future.edu/apply.
The application deadline for the Fall semester is August 15th.
The application deadline for the Spring semester is December 15th.
Eligibility criteria: Must have completed high school with 75% grades or equivalent.
International students must provide a valid English proficiency test score (IELTS 6.5+ or TOEFL 90+).

=== ACADEMIC CALENDAR ===
The Fall semester starts on September 1st and ends on December 20th.
The Spring semester starts on January 15th and ends on May 10th.
Summer term is optional and runs from June 1st to July 30th.

=== COURSES OFFERS ===
1. Bachelor of Computer Science (BCS)
   - Duration: 4 years
   - Focus: AI, Robotics, Quantum Computing
   - Credits: 120

2. Bachelor of Space Engineering (BSE)
   - Duration: 4 years
   - Focus: Propulsion, Orbital Mechanics
   - Credits: 130

3. Master of Data Science (MDS)
   - Duration: 2 years
   - Prerequisite: Bachelor's in STEM field
   
=== FEES & SCHOLARSHIPS ===
Tuition fee per credit is $200.
Housing fee: $500 per month (On-campus dorms).
Meal plan: $300 per month.
Scholarships are available for top 10% students based on entrance exam scores.
Financial aid forms can be submitted via the student portal.

=== FACILITIES ===
Library: Open 24/7 during exam weeks. Normal hours: 8 AM - 10 PM.
Gym: Free for all registered students. Located in Building C.
Cafeteria: Serving organic and synthetic food options.
Health Center: Located in Building A, Ground Floor.

=== IT SUPPORT ===
For WiFi access, use your student ID as username and DOB as password.
IT Helpdesk is located in the Library, 2nd floor.
Email support: it@future.edu.

=== INTERNSHIPS ===
Students are eligible for internships after completing 60 credits.
The implementation of the internship program partners with top tech firms like Google, SpaceX, and Neuralink.
The internship duration is typically 3 months and requires full-time commitment.

=== CODE OF CONDUCT ===
Usage of AI tools for assignments is permitted but must be disclosed.
